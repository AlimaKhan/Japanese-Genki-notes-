Lesson 2:Kore (これ)・Sore (それ)・Are (あれ)・Dore (どれ)

Explanation

これ → A thing close to the speaker (“this thing here”).

それ → Something that is close to person you are talking to (“that thing infront of you”).

あれ → A thing that is neither close to the speaker nor the listner.(“that over there”).

どれ → Which one?

Examples

これはペンです。 → This is a pen.

それはほんです。 → That is a book.

あれはやまです。 → That (over there) is a mountain.

どれがあなたのかばんですか。 → Which one is your bag?

Practice

Fill in the blanks:

  1. ＿＿はペンです。 (This is a pen.)
  2. ＿＿はほんです。 (That is a book near you.)
  3. ＿＿はくるまです。 (That car over there.)
  4. ＿＿があなたのかさですか。 (Which one is your umbrella?)
